Veteran Artist Residencies, a non-profit venture

This organization provides paid artistic residencies to post-9/11 combat veterans dedicated to transcending their experiences through the arts.

Nate Hankes, the founder, discovered the healing power of art while writing his bestselling travel adventure and war memoir, Waking Up On the Appalachian Trail. He has dedicated a cottage on his property to provide his fellow veterans the opportunity to reclaim their joy.

The Mission

Between 2020 and 2023, the Veteran Artist Residencies non-profit intends to support four veterans by offering each a five-month paid artist residency and a free place to live.

What type of artists are we looking for?

  • Novelists.
  • Memoirists.
  • Painters.
  • Poets.
  • Screen play writers.
  • All art forms will be considered.

During the application process, we look for potential and ambition. A lot can be accomplished in five months, and we want our veteran artists to show up ready to immerse themselves in their artistic endeavors.
