At the Veteran Artist Residencies non-profit, we believe all Americans benefit when veteran voices are heard.

Unfortunately, many of today's returning veterans struggle to find stability, belonging, and economic self-sufficiency. In such circumstances, it seems sort of silly to dedicate your time toward working on your masterpiece.

The mission of Veteran Artist Residencies is to tap into this untapped potential by providing a space where post-9/11 combat veterans can live free from survival concerns and focus solely on their art.

The Mission

Between 2020 and 2023, the Veteran Artist Residencies non-profit intends to support four veterans by offering each a five-month fully-paid artist residency in beautiful and inspiring Humboldt County, California.

What type of artists are we looking for?

  • Novelists.
  • Memoirists.
  • Poets.
  • Graphic Novelists.
  • Screenplay writers.
  • All forms of writing will be considered.

During the application process, we look for potential and ambition. A lot can be accomplished in five months, and we want our veteran artists to show up ready to immerse themselves in their artistic endeavors.
