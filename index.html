Veteran Artist Residencies

An Ink People Center for the Arts DreamMaker Project

We provide post-9/11 combat veterans with paid artistic residencies.

Military service has shaped and influenced generations of America's most influencial and powerful artists and storytellers.

From Pulitzer Prizes to Hollywood blockbusters, these artists draw inspiration from their experiences in the military—and often have something meaningful to communicate.

Unfortunately, many of today's returning veterans struggle to find stability, belonging, and economic self-sufficiency. In such circumstances, it seems irresponsible to dedicate your time toward working on your masterpiece. This is a tradegy.

Our Mission

Between 2020 and 2023, the Veteran Artist Residencies non-profit intends to support four veterans by offering each a five-month fully-paid artist residency in beautiful and inspiring Humboldt County, California.

What type of artists are we looking for?

  • Novelists.
  • Memoirists.
  • Poets.
  • Graphic Novelists.
  • Screenplay writers.
  • All forms of writing will be considered.

During the application process, we look for potential and ambition. A lot can be accomplished in five months, and we want our veteran artists to show up ready to immerse themselves in their artistic endeavors.

We Need Your Support

Your donations go directly toward monthly stipends and living expenses for veteran artists.

Our mission is limited in scope, and our requirements are modest.

Over the lifetime of this project, we need to raise $60,000, or $15,000 per veteran.

If 1,000 people donate $60, we're fully funded. In the age of the interent, this could happen in a short amount of time!
