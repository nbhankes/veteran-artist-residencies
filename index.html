The Mission

Between 2020 and 2023, the Veteran Artist Residencies non-profit intends to support four veterans by offering each a five-month fully-paid artist residency in beautiful and inspiring Humboldt County, California.

What type of artists are we looking for?

  • Novelists.
  • Memoirists.
  • Poets.
  • Graphic Novelists.
  • Screenplay writers.
  • All forms of writing will be considered.

During the application process, we look for potential and ambition. A lot can be accomplished in five months, and we want our veteran artists to show up ready to immerse themselves in their artistic endeavors.

Why art?

Without art, I could have been a statistic.

Everyday 20 veterans commit suicide.

I came home from Iraq in 2008 confused and angry.

Without the support of friends and family, I could have easily become a statistic.

I found purpose in my art.

I started writing. Just stream of consciousness at first.

But then I decided I’d write an actual book. I had a mission.

I spent years working on various drafts, toiling in solitude and studying the craft.

In 2020, I released my travel and war memoir, Waking Up On the Appalachian Trail, and it quickly became an Amazon best seller.

The artistic process saves lives.

Writing about the pain and confusion helped me process my wartime trauma.

The clarity made the wounds lose their charge and power over me.

Combined with yoga and mindfulness meditation, the inner transformation couldn’t be more drastic.

Now I'm thriving. And now I know this process will help other veterans.

Your generosity helps veterans find peace.
